Rose Photo

Rose Photo was the final project of my web illustrations class at Weber State University. We spent the semester learning and practicing creating vector images with various drawing tools. We learned techniques for applying color, attributes, styles, and effects to illustrations. Additionally, I created, edited, and formated type to add to my illustrations.

For this final project, I took all the skills I had gained and made polished designs for a fictional business, Rose Photo. These designs would give the business everything they needed to have a strong foundation for the look and feel of their brand across the internet, including social media.
