Roonie's Ice Cream

Roonie’s Ice Cream received a full rework of their website. Efforts started with communication directly with the owner, Stacy, to determine goals and needs. A mock up was made, then an initial work up of the entire website. At this point, input came from the owner and other web experts. Fine tuning was done and the website was finalized.
